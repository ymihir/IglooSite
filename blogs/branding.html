Why your business branding and Identity makes all the diference?

Before diving into the topic directly,i would like to discuss about the user persona i.e demographics and psychographics of current Gen Y-proudly called the age of millenials,which means people born after 1982.You may agree wih me that smart phone has become our best friend and sometimes to an extent ignoring people around us,The millineal users use internet to gather information primarly,make use of technology for making an enquiry,perform a transaction etc and are very proactive to report/critise and spread messages which effects image of a brand or a pubclic personality either directly or indirectly.

Lets face it, the best way to reach out to Geny-Y or making the Gen-Y to reach out to you is defining the right brand strategies,shoutout to them and be heard.As per the PMAR report 2017,it is evident that brands are increasing thier ad spends digitally narrowing thier focus to specific audience by getting to know thier customer user profiles.

These insights above help us make better decisions regarding our ad spends and digital is surely going to rock in coming years making the businesess retrospect the traditional ways of promoting their services/products.Businesess customers are surey online,The brand identiy of the business makes a huge impact on the purchase desicion of the user,the customer nowadays is empowered with data that enables him make better purchase decision at the time of need.

Branding and Identity plays an important role in driving sales,consumer loyalty,credibiity of their products and services.Business need to rethink about having an appealing branding and identity which is in simple terms thier name,logo,typeface,tone and tagline.It should appeal to their audience when they are making a purchase and brand name gets subconsiosly recalled when the customer is looking for similar service or product.Having a logo and a tag line which resonates with customer beliefs thus giving a clear edge in the marketplace among its competitors.

Internet penetration is going to increase manifold in coming years and surely influences purchase desicions and perception of making purchases of the customer.Its high time Small and Mid sized business need to act on the importance of having a unique brand identity to reach out to new customer as well as retaining the existing customers.The advantage of having an identity is it encodes a subconsious belief sysemts to be truly sucessful.

Having a strong branding and identity has advantages such as brand awareness and consistency in the marketplace.The more customers know about your brand the more you are alive in the market place and customer becomes loyal towards the brand and becomes a follower.Best example of brands having a consistent brand identity are Apple and Coca-cola.The more a business works on its branding and Identity the more its seeds a subconcious belief system in consumers mind.

To conclude ,if you want to succeed and sustain in the market place, better work on your branding and Identity.
