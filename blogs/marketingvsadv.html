Do you the know the difference between marketing and advertising?

Advertising by definition is a means of communication with the users of a product or service. Advertisements are messages paid for by those who send them and are intended to inform or influence people who receive them, as defined by the Advertising Association of the UK.

Advertising is always present in many forms which we notice in day to day lives may it be a TV ad, radio jingle, print ad etc. We notice that different businesses promote their product or service in different communication mediums to attract customers buy their product/service.

Many people mistake advertising and marketing are same, to make it more easy and put it in simple terms,In order to promote a product we need to devise a strategy/plan on how we should communicate to the targeted audience for this ,we conceptualise an idea, plan execution through different communications channels.Once,we are clear with the message that we need to pass it to customers we need to define a communication strategy and promote it using any one/many available communication channels to reach out to identified marketing segments, Now this is called marketing where promotion is involved.

An agency role is to come up with ideas a communication roadmap for the businesses product or service and efficiently identify the right channels to ensure message is reached to its customers.

Effective Advertising is all about crafting a message that has strong CTA and marketing is something which makes the message visible to businesses identified targeted customers.
